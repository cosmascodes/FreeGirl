What we do

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Psycho-social Support

We provide psycho-social support to school-going girls who became pregnant during the global Corona pandemic season when the government closed all schools.

Empowerment workshops

We hold monthly empowerment workshops for girls where we focus on life-skills training, purpose awareness, and self-confidence development.

Sanitary Pads Program

We provide sanitary pads to underprivileged girls in the community once a month so that they do not stay out of school due to a lack of sanitary protection.

Talents & Enterpreneurship

We facilitate talent and entrepreneurship mentorship programs. We link our girls to seasoned mentors who coach and support them and act as role models in various areas.

Top-notch Education

We strive to ensure that our girls pursue ongoing education and professional career training by mobilizing for resources to enable them to pay school fees in situations where their parents are unable to afford this.

Advovacy & Social change

We ensure that the voices of the girls are heard on both local and global platforms; We help them to access information about issues and initiatives concerning women.

Our Projects

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Adopt a Girl Education

Fully or partially support a girl by providing school fees to enable them get quality education. The package also covers variables depending on the fee structure, educational opportunity and other personal requirements to enable the individual to attend and participate.

Adopt a Girl Business Fund

Provide the start-up capital needs of a selected young woman who has successfully undergone our mentorship program and has shown the potential to run a successful business enterprise-starting small. The package will be dependent on the business plan costings for the business.

Adopt a Program

Partner with any program by paying for its facilitation, leadership and overheads. Example programs include programs focused on the following; psycho-social support, empowerment workshops, talent, and entrepreneurship mentorship programs.
